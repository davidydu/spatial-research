# D-14 Angle 10: Recommendation Matrix for Fork/ForkJoin/PrimitiveBox

## Decision Frame

Q-140 asks for operational semantics for `Fork`, `ForkJoin`, and `PrimitiveBox` in the Rust/HLS scheduler (`20 - Research Notes/40 - Decision Queue.md:63-65`). `ForkJoin` comes from `ParallelPipe` and behaves like a parallel region with an all-child join; `Fork` comes from `Switch`/`IfThenElse` and behaves like selected-branch execution; `PrimitiveBox` comes from `SpatialBlackboxImpl`, not a Fringe controller schedule (`[[01-control-metadata-effective-schedule]]`; `[[03-api-ir-origins]]`). The source enum contains all three beside `Sequenced`, `Pipelined`, and `Streaming`, but effective schedule queries preserve these special tags while only collapsing single-control `Pipelined` to `Sequenced` (`src/spatial/metadata/control/ControlData.scala:7-14`; `src/spatial/metadata/control/package.scala:193-219`; `[[10 - Control]]`).

## Cross-Decision Coupling

This decision should not make D-15 or D-22 silently disappear. `ParallelPipe` is the source-level surface behind `ForkJoin`, so a serial simulator can be retained only as a labelled compatibility mode, not as the scheduler's definition (`20 - Research Notes/40 - Decision Queue.md:67-69`; `[[03-api-ir-origins]]`). Likewise, bounded FIFO/stream behavior belongs in D-22, but `ForkJoin` cannot be cycle-faithful without some answer for child back-pressure, skipped children, and deadlock/overflow diagnostics (`20 - Research Notes/40 - Decision Queue.md:95-97`). D-06/D-08/D-21 also matter because HLS reports, accepted II, and latency provenance determine whether the model's max-child and branch-weight equations remain estimates or become calibrated facts (`[[D-06]]`; `[[D-08]]`).

## Preliminary Recommendation

Preliminarily choose **hybrid modes with HLS-native synthesis semantics**. Define canonical scheduler semantics as: `ForkJoin` starts all enabled children for a logical iteration and joins after all non-skipped children complete; `Fork` evaluates branch selects, executes the selected case under one-hot or documented priority semantics, and diagnoses multi-true ambiguity; `PrimitiveBox` is an atomic primitive/blackbox leaf requiring declared effects, latency/II, and either a Rust reference model or HLS lowering. Keep Scalagen serial execution only as `compat_scalagen_serial` for regression. Reject opaque metadata as a default; allow it only as an explicit unsupported/fallback diagnostic.
